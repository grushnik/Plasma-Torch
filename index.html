What is Plasma Nitrification?

Plasma nitrification uses high-energy plasma to fix atmospheric nitrogen into reactive nitrogen species, enabling localized nitric acid or nitrate production for on-site fertilizer.

What is Plasma Gasification?

Plasma gasification converts organic and carbon-rich waste streams—such as biomass, plastics, or municipal solid waste—into a clean synthesis gas (syngas) primarily composed of hydrogen and carbon monoxide. Operating at very high plasma temperatures, the process ensures near-complete breakdown of waste, minimizes toxic byproducts, and enables recovery of useful energy and chemicals. This syngas can then be upgraded into fuels, fertilizers, or other valuable products, while simultaneously reducing emissions and landfill dependency.
