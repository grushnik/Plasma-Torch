What is Plasma Nitrification?

Plasma nitrification uses high-energy plasma to fix atmospheric nitrogen into reactive nitrogen species, enabling localized nitric acid or nitrate production for on-site fertilizer. When coupled with plasma gasification, waste streams can be valorized into syngas and chemicals while reducing emissions.
